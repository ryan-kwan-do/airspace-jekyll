About Me

Welcome to my home on the web! I use Systems thinking, philosophy, personal mastery, and holistic organizing in the service of team learning and discovery.

Here you can find out more about working with me on change projects, my current research, and my writings. I work with organizations to learn about complex problems and what can be done at them. My research interests lie in the challenges relating to the 4th Industrial revolution—technological change, big data, changing societal directions, and climate change, and how to navigate them through systems thinking and other participatory tools.
